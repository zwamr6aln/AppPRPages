<h2 id="概要"> Overview</h2>
<p> Just a Shogi board where you can move the pieces freely. app for iPhone, iPad, Mac, Apple Watch and Apple TV.</p>
<p> Pieces move freely. You can delete or add any piece.</p>
<p> You can customize the font of the pieces and change the English notation to make your own Shogi board.</p>
<p><img src="screenshot1200w.png" width="600"></p>
<h3 id="shareplay"> SharePlay</h3>
<p> Multiple players can operate one Shogi board at the same time.</p>
<p> You can share the Shogi board with your friends over the Internet via "FaceTime" or "Messages" apps.</p>
<p> The SharePlay function is designed for various use cases such as "solving Tsume-Shogi with a large group of friends" as well as "a simple game between two players".</p>
<p> You can also start SharePlay by simply placing two iPhones close to each other and pressing a button once. (Limited to iPhones with iOS 17 or later)</p>
<p><img src="SharePlay600w.png" width="300"></p>
<h3 id="マルチプラットフォーム対応"> Multi-platform support</h3>
<p> Compatible with iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch, Mac, and Apple TV.</p>
<p> Stations are synchronized between devices via iCloud.</p>
