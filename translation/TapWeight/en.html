TapWeight

Register your weight data in the "Health Care" app as fast as possible!

Overview

This is an application for registering weight data to the Apple "Health Care" application pre-installed on the iPhone in the fastest possible time (for manual operation).

Intended users / use cases

  • Do not have a smart scale.
  • They measure their weight and body fat percentage on a scale on a daily basis.
  • Considering registering the results manually in the "Health Care" application immediately after each measurement on the scale.

Options

  • Unit
    • kg kilogram
    • lbs Pounds
    • st Stone
  • Input unit can be changed from 100g to 50g
  • BMI can be automatically calculated and registered at the same time
  • Percentage of body fat can be registered at the same time
  • Ability to change the date and time of registration

Other features

  • One-tap launch of the Apple "Health Care" app from within this app
  • Registration can be canceled immediately after registration
  • History function in the device for operation check and simple backup

Caution

This app cannot read, view, or manage past data in the "Health Care" app. This app is intended only for registering data to the "Health Care" app. Please check the registered data on the "Health Care" application.

Background

People frequently measure their weight and body fat percentage on a daily basis using a scale. Many iPhone users register their weight and body fat data on the "Health Care" application.

The best solution is to use a smart scale that works with the Health Care app and automatically stores measurements, but they can be expensive and inaccessible. Manual data registration is possible in the "Health Care" app, but unfortunately, the "Health Care" app is not designed for daily manual registration of measurements. Therefore, manually entering data that occurs continuously on a daily basis, such as weight measurements, can be a very time-consuming and stressful experience.

We developed this app to solve such problems.
