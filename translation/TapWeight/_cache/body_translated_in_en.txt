<h2 id="概要"> Overview</h2>
<p> This is an application for iPhone/Apple Watch only.</p>
<p> Intended users / use cases</p>
<ul>
<li><p> Do not have a smart scale.</p></li>
<li><p> They measure their weight and body fat percentage on a scale on a daily basis.</p></li>
<li><p> Considering registering the measurement results manually in the "Health Care" app immediately after each measurement on the scale.</p></li>
</ul>
<p> Options</p>
<ul>
<li><p> BMI can be automatically calculated and registered at the same time.</p></li>
<li><p> Body fat percentage can be registered at the same time.</p></li>
<li><p> Lean body mass (LBM) can be automatically calculated and registered at the same time.</p></li>
<li><p> Input unit can be changed from 100g to 50g.</p></li>
<li><p> Compatible with Apple Watch.</p></li>
<li><p> Reminder notification function</p></li>
<li><p> Ability to change the date and time of registration.</p></li>
<li><p> Option to hide advertisement banner (in-app purchase). (In-app purchase)</p></li>
<li><p> Units: kg, lbs, st</p></li>
</ul>
<p> Miscellaneous</p>
<ul>
<li><p> One tap to launch Apple's "Health Care" app from within the app.</p></li>
<li><p> Registration can be canceled immediately after registration.</p></li>
</ul>
<p> Concept</p>
<p> People frequently measure their weight and body fat percentage on a daily basis using scales. Many iPhone users register their weight and body fat data on the "Health Care" app. The best solution is to use a smart scale that works with the "Health Care" app and automatically stores measurements, but they can be expensive and inaccessible. Manual data registration is possible in the "Health Care" app, but unfortunately the "Health Care" app is not designed for daily manual registration of measurement data. This makes manually entering data that occurs continuously on a daily basis, such as weight measurements, a very time-consuming and stressful experience. This app was developed to solve such problems.</p>
<p> This app cannot read, view, or manage past data in the "Health Care" app. This app is only for registering data to the "Health Care" app. Please check the registered data on the "Health Care" application.</p>
