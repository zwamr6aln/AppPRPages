<h3 id="„Éö„Éº„Ç∏„ÇÅ„Åè„Çä„ÅÆ‰ªïÁµÑ„Åø"> How the page turn works</h3>
<p><img src="preview.gif" class="except_printing"></p>
<p><img src="flow.svg" width="540"></p>
<p> When you close your eyelids a little longer, the page is sent forward, and when you open your eyes, the page has already moved to the next page. Normal blinking does not cause the page to turn over.</p>
<p> Blinking is detected by the device's built-in camera and sensors.</p>
<p> As with general e-book applications, you can also turn pages by tapping or swiping the screen.</p>

<li> Supports import of PDF and ZIP files.</li>
</ul>
