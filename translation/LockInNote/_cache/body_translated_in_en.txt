<h2 id="概要"> Overview</h2>
<p> This is a note-taking app specialized for widgets, and is available for iPhone/iPad/Mac/Apple Watch.</p>
<p> When you want to take a note, you can quickly write it out from the widget and check it on the widget at any time!</p>
<p> The memo note design on the widget can be flexibly customized to meet various purposes.</p>
<h3 id="マルチプラットフォーム対応"> Multi-platform support</h3>
<ul>
<li> iPhone: Home screen, Lock screen, Standby (iOS 17)</li>
<li> iPad: Home screen, Lock screen (iPadOS 17)</li>
<li> Mac: Notification center, desktop (macOS 14)</li>
<li> Apple Watch: Complication, Smart Stack (watchOS 10)</li>
</ul>
