<h2 id="概要"> Overview</h2>
<p> This is a note app specialized for the lock screen widget, an app for iPhone/Apple Watch.</p>
<p> When you want to take a note, you can write a note right on the lock screen and check the note on the lock screen at any time!</p>
<p> It can be linked with Apple Watch.</p>
<p> The design of the note on the lock screen can be flexibly customized to meet various purposes of "leaving notes" and "displaying text on the lock screen.</p>
<p> Tapping the widget immediately launches the keyboard to edit the note.</p>
