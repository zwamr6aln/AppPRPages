LockInNote

A notebook application specialized in widgets!

Overview

This is a note-taking app specialized for widgets, and is available for iPhone/iPad/Mac/Apple Watch.

When you want to take a note, you can quickly write it out from the widget and check it on the widget at any time!

The memo note design on the widget can be flexibly customized to meet various purposes.

Multi-platform support

  • iPhone: Home screen, Lock screen, Standby (iOS 17)
  • iPad: Home screen, Lock screen (iPadOS 17)
  • Mac: Notification center, desktop (macOS 14)
  • Apple Watch: Complication, Smart Stack (watchOS 10)
