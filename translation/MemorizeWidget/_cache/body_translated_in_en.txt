<h2 id="概要"> Overview</h2>
<p> Display your memorandum book on your home screen or lock screen using the widget function. App for iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch, and Mac.</p>
<ul>
<li> Two modes: "stand-alone mode" and "random mode.</li>
<li> In "Random Mode," the notes displayed change every 5 minutes</li>
<li> Lock screen widget support (iOS16 only)</li>
<li> Memorandum data can be imported from text or files (csv, tsv, txt, etc.)</li>
<li> Linkage with OS built-in dictionary</li>
<li> Customizable search function</li>
<li> Synchronize data between devices via iCloud</li>
</ul>
<p><img src="screenshot1200w.png" width="600"></p>
<p> Optional</p>
<ul>
<li> Display "comment" as well as "title" of notes on widgets (only for home screen widgets/lock screen rectangular widgets)</li>
<li> Display of multiple notes</li>
</ul>
<p> Multi-platform support</p>
<ul>
<li> iPhone: Lock screen widget (iOS16 or later), Home screen widget</li>
<li> iPad: Home screen widget</li>
<li> Apple Watch: Complication</li>
<li> Mac: Notification Center widget</li>
</ul>
<p> Recommended for the latest iPhone 14 Pro model!</p>
<p> With the iPhone 14 Pro with Always On Display, you can check your notepad without touching your iPhone!</p>
